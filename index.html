Introduction

About Origami as creative physical activity

Origami, also called paper folding, art of folding objects out of paper to create both two-dimensional and three-dimensional subjects. The word origami (from Japanese oru [“to fold”] and kami [“paper”]) has become the generic description of this art form.
Discover

Benefit of origami

Origami provides both mental and physical stimulus with exercise. Use of the hands stimulates areas of the brain, improving short-term memory. It offers a calming and relaxing effect by following a simple step-by step process, which can be repeated and taught again and again, until success is achieved.

User testing

One of the most important aspects of the design process for me is constantly testing the design and iterating based on feedback. So we tested low fidelity mockup to see if the user journey flow and interactions made sense before spending time moving them into higher fidelity.

After user testing, we received unexpected feedback from the user. So we decided to reanalyze the research data and iterate our final design.
Deliver

Key features

Simple step-by-step introduction

Providing clear instruction

To get started, we have provided a simple introduction to help user understand how to use our service. By clicking on the "Continue" button, user can learn about each step of the process.
Providing motivation

Customized your origami

User can customize their origami to make designs truly unique. When user select a design, they can choose from a variety of paper colors, patterns and sound effects.

After completing customization, user can send invitation with tutorial. Providing users with step-by-step instructions that are easy to follow, ensuring that they understand each step.
Sharing origami

Order the Invitation kit

Based on what user create origami design, user gets to choose to have the customized material kit delivered to their household and get ready for the party activity.
